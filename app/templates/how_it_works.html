How It Works

The system processes live video, detects hand gestures, predicts the corresponding token, and communicates the result to the integrated AI chatbot for a natural conversational response.

1

Capture

The webcam stream is captured using OpenCV and transmitted in real time through Flask’s video route.

2

Detect

MediaPipe extracts precise hand landmarks and keypoints for gesture classification.

3

Predict

A Deep Learning MLP model interprets the landmarks and maps them to a recognized signs.

4

Respond

The output is displayed as text and passed to the integrated chatbot, which generates a conversational reply.
